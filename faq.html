Repair or Replace?

Many modern large-scale manufacturers of furniture are focused on quantity, not quality. This makes the furniture cheaper, but also weaker and with a shorter life-span. However, If your frame is strong and of good quality, it can last for decades. When good frames get damaged, they are usually worth repairing. When bad frames get damaged, they are usually worth nothing. We can work from the frame up to completely rebuild and restore your furniture.

Please contact us for advice if you are unsure if your damaged furniture can be repaired
