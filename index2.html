The Upholstery Studio offers a full upholstery service to revive, reinvigorate and reinvent your furniture.

We are a family business, run by Detlef and Stefan Schulmeistrat. We specialise in repairing, recovering and reupholstering all types of furniture. We are based in Co Wicklow and service Wicklow, Dublin and all surrounding areas.

With over 35 years experience providing durable, affordable luxury in Antique, Vintage and Contemporary styles, we are dedicated to our craft and to our customers. Using traditional techniques and materials of the highest quality, our aim is to ensure that your project, however big or small, is completed to your satisfaction, within your budget and in your timescale.

For more information or to arrange a free no-obligation estimate please contact us either by e-mail or telephone.
